OUTSOURCED BOOKKEEPING


In-house bookkeeping can become expensive – and although that bookkeeper sitting in your office doesn’t seem to bother too much, there are costs associated with that space, equipment and software they are using. Think of the things you can do with that space, perhaps there is a need to expand your work space, or it’s a perfect location for a new floral designer that can generate income, not an expense!

Let’s not even talk about employing a full-time bookkeeper! According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median annual wage for a bookkeeper was $38,390 in May 2016. Unless your shop’s annual revenue is well above $2 million dollars, employing a full-time bookkeeper is not cost effective. This doesn’t even factor any other associated costs related to industry specific training. An outsourced bookkeeper can work in their own space and use their own equipment, thus saving you time and money (isn’t this the goal?).

Yeah, thanks to the cloud – it’s a thing!
