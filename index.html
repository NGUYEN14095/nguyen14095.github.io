HOW IT WORKS

After joining our team, we’ll send you a scanner and set it up for you. Then you can simply scan your receipts, bills, & invoices to us and we’ll send you financial statements at the intervals you need them. Although we won’t be printing and sending your bill payments, we’ll send them to our payment processor where you’ll log in and approve each bill – keeping you in full control of your bank account.
